SOUP BASE

Viet-Thai Broth

Rich, sour, and slightly spice broth. Consist of lemongrass, galangal, chili, tamarind paste, and more.

Sour Broth

Vibrant sweet and soup broth. A tamarind-based broth with fish sauce, sugar, and fried garlic oil. Paired best with white rice.

Pho Broth

A classic Vietnamese broth. Simmered with charred onions, ginger, beef marrow, and toasted spices. It's brewed for 6-12 hours.

Crab Broth

A bright red color with a tangy, slightly sweet, and salty flavor profile. Made from crab extract, shrimp paste, tomatoes, and spices. Tangy flavor.

Curry Broth

Rich and creamy best paired with noodles. Coconut milk, yellow curry powder, lemongrass, fish sauce, and more.

Mushroom Broth

A rich vegetarian alternative broth. Crafted by shittake mushrooms, charred onions, ginger, and more.
