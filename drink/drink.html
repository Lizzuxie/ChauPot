CHAU'S POT: OUR CURATED DRINK

We also provide delicious drinks to pair your food or just itself! Explore 10 unique drinks.



Can't decide what to drink? We recommend Ca Phe Muoi!



Precaution: Our coffee is very strong as it has 3-4 shots of espresso!

Vietnamese Coffee

Bold, slow-brewed robusta coffee with condensed milk, making it creamy. Its coffee is rich, smooth, and perfectly sweetened for coffee lovers.

Ca Phe Muoi

Deep, robust coffee topped with velverty salted foam for a sweet-salty finish. A classic drink from Vietnam.

Jasmine Matcha Latte

Matcha meets jasmine infused milk. With ceremonial grade matcha and homemade jasmine infused milk, its flavors are rich, smooth, earthy, and floral.

Thai Milk Tea

Strong brewed thai tea, sweetened with evaporated milk and condensed milk. It's bold, aromatic, and satisyingly rich.

Pandan Latte

Fragrant pandan infused into creamy milk creates a subtly sweet, floral aroma reminiscent of vanilla. Followed by a shot of espresso and housemade pandan syrup, creating a fragrant delight for any time.

Mango Pomelo Sago

Tropical mango blend is combined with pomelo pulp, chewy sago pearls, and coconut milk. Sweet, fruity, and texturally fun in every sip.

Avocado Smoothie

Thick, creamy avocado blended with milk for a rich and lightly sweet taste. Smooth and refreshing all at once. Oatmilk alternative is available.

Fruit Tea

Bright, fruity flavors mixed with refreshing oolong tea. The fruits consist of passionfruit, dragonfruit, orange, pinapple, and strawberry. Sweet and tangy notes.

Che Thai

Our signature, classic Vietnamese dessert drink. Colorful mix of tropical fruits like longan and jackfruit, mixed with grass jelly, tapioca, and finishing off with coconut milk for a refreshing taste.

Ube Latte

Delicious, creamy milk with a sweet, nutty ube paste for a smooth and velvety drink. Oatmilk alternative is available.
