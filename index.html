About Us

Inspired by the communal warmth of shared meals and Vietnamese comfort, we as a family set out to create something that blends creativity with comfort.

Our signature "build-your-own-lau" brings a twist to traditional hotpot. Drawing inspiration from malatang, we invite you to create a meal that's entirely your own.

For lighter options, our banh baos offer a perfect street-style snack. And to complete the experience, we serve cafe-style drinks infused with Vietnamese flair.

Bring your friends, family, or yourself to gather, sip, and settle in.
